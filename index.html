"Anugerah Christian School (ACS) Sobaham"

Berdiri pada tanggal 12 Januari Tahun 2015 di Kampung Owitlep, Distrik Sobaham, Kabupaten Yahukimo, Papua. Sekolah memiliki beberapa layanan, diantaranya : PAUD (Pendidikan Anak Usia Dini), SD (Sekolah Dasar) dan SMP (Sekolah Menengah Pertama).

Kepala Sekolah

Nick E. Imbiri

Saya percaya melalui pendidikan, anak-anak di pelosok Papua bisa bermimpi dan juga meraih mimpinya.
Papua dikenal sebagai salah satu Provinsi terkaya, sekaligus termiskin. Karena minimnya pendidikan terutama di daerah terpencil, saya berharap melalui kehadiran saya di Sobaham, saya dapat membagikan pentingnya pendidikan kepada para siswa. Karena saya percaya bahwa setiap orang bisa sukses dengan caranya sendiri, tapi pendidikan adalah salah satu cara mudah untuk mencapai impian mereka.

Mr. Nick E. Imbiri

"I believe through education, the children in remote area in Papua would be able to dream and also achieve their dreams"

Teaching's been my passion since I was young. Being able to teach to at the highlands of Papua is such a blessed for me. Not just because I finally realized my dream but I also being able to teach the future of Papua.

Papua has been known as one of the richest Province yet, the poorest at the same time. Because of lack of education especially at the remote area, I wish that through my presence here in Sobaham, I could share the importance of education to the students. Because I believe that everyone could be successful in their own way, but education is one the easy way to achieve their dreams.

"Life is Sharing"

Being a teacher means giving another chance to their students to have another shot in their life. They also can bring out their students potential so they can have more bright future.

Ms. Irma S

"Live would have more meaning, when You touch other's life."

Teaching is one of my hobbies; because through teaching I could meet with all of potential youth. Another reason why I want to teach because I want to touch other people's life. Because, I realize that life would be more living when you actually touch other people's life.

When I got a chance to go to Papua to teach, I actually remember back when I was young. I was living in a family that believe, me and my siblings couldn't make it to go to college. Yet, God's grace was real for us. I made it and actually finished my college on 2019.

During my Sophomore Year at College, I accept Jesus as my one and only savior. That's when I started to saw things differently. God was real, He's actually there to help me and my siblings.

Since God touched my heart, I decided to give my life to serve His purpose. Papua has been behind in Education for years, therefore being there was one of my dreams.

I wish that being here in Sobaham and live with the kids, I could touch their life and they could be brave to striving forward.
